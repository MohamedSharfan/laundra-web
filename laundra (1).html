Frequently Asked

Got
Questions?

Standard orders are completed within 24–48 hours from pickup. Our Express 6-hour service is available for urgent orders and costs a small premium. You'll receive real-time updates throughout the entire process.
Click "Book Pickup" on our website or app. Select your service type, estimate your load weight, choose your preferred pickup window, add any special instructions, and confirm. Our riders will arrive within your chosen window.
We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. Payment is processed securely at the time of booking. Final pricing is adjusted based on actual weight at the hub, with any difference refunded or charged automatically.
Click "Become a Rider" and complete the registration form. After a quick background verification (usually 24 hours), you'll get access to the Rider Portal where you can start accepting jobs and earn flexible income on your own schedule.
All garments are insured for their declared value during processing. In the rare event of damage, contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery and we'll initiate a claim. We cover up to $500 per order on standard services.
